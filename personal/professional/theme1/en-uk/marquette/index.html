Marquette Corners

< >

Alumni Memorial Union

The Alumni Memorial Union, or AMU, is the first building that I visited when I arrived at Marquette in 2017 with my parents. This building has a Marquette-themed shop, a cafe, a bank, and more. I love the design of this building, which carries history but does not appear old.

Lalumiere Language Hall

This is the building in which I learned English and Spanish. I really like the design. It looks like a honeycomb, isn't it? Oh, I also had anthropology here. I enjoyed all the courses I took in this building.

Raynor Memorial Libraries

The library is where I study on my own or with my friends. The library is big and I worked as a digital technician here for one semester. This is my favorite building on campus.

Cudahy Hall

This is inside Cudahy Hall. I had a few mathematics and information systems classes here. I like this building because it is for computer science. I fell in love with programming since I touched Python.
