--- layout: research-en-uk title: Data science Programme ---

Data science is one of the areas of focus in my research. The Data Science Programme was launched to tackle challenges in data. I learn to manipulate and analyse data to retrieve actionable insights, whether that’s in business, science or other fields.

I’m keen to master computational technologies such as programming languages Python, R and GNU Octave and software programs such as Alteryx, Tableau and Power BI to extract, manipulate, analyse and visualise data. I believe data has much value to be unlocked that could be of immense help for business. I hope this Programme will help me get to where I hope to be with data technologies.
