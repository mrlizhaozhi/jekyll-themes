Social media

Please follow me on the following platforms:

  • LinkedIn

    I maintain a public profile on LinkedIn. This is where I update my career progression and communicate with other professionals. Feel free to reach out on LinkedIn!

  • GitHub

    This is where I put all my open-source projects. I'm specialised in web applications, data science, artificial intelligence, and computer graphics. Please follow me on GitHub to explore open-source projects on these subjects.

  • CodePen

    As a professional web developer, I build lots and lots of websites and web-based applications. CodePen is a good place to showcase my front-end skills.

  • Kaggle

    Kaggle is where I get data and tackle data analysis challenges. Follow my Kaggle profile to learn more. I'm working on a few projects in Kaggle Notebook right now.

  • Tableau Public

    Tableau is an awesome data visualisation software. Tableau Public is the free edition of the software. Here, I build a lot of dashboards to analyse a range of issues and retrieve actionable insights.
